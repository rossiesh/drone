## General information

- printer used: Elegoo Neptune 4
- filament: Elegoo Pla+
- black Pla+ for the landing gear and plates for the electronics
- white Pla+ for the body parts and arms due to better heat reflection than black Pla+
- print arm.3mf four times
- print cable_holder.3mf four times
- print landing_gear_long.3mf four times
- print everything else one time
- use drone_arms.curaprofile only for the arms
- use drone_electronics_plates.curaprofile for:
  - e_plate_bottom.3mf
  - e_plate_top.3mf
- use drone_main_body.curaprofile for:
  - antenna_holder.3mf
  - bottom_plate.3mf
  - cable_holder.3mf
  - landing_gear_long.3mf
  - landing_gear_short.3mf
  - middle_plate.3mf
