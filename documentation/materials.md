## Materials

- FC and controller: 2x XIAO ESP32 S3 *(~15€ for the three-pack)*
- IMU: MPU6500 *(~3€)*
- barometer: MS5611 *(~5€)*
- ESC: Sequre Blueson A2 (comes with power cables, XT60 connector and capacitor) *(~30€)*
- motors: 4x iFlight XING-E Pro 2207 2450KV (screws and self-locking nuts included) *(~35€)* or similar
- props: 4x iFlight Nazgul F5 *(~5€)*
- battery: e.g. Goldbat 4s 1500mAh LiPo *(~35€ for a two-pack)* (an additional LiPo charger is required)
- generic DC-DC 3A adjustable buck converter, e.g. MP1584EN *(~4€ for the five-pack)*
- 2.4GHz rod antenna (2.81dBi) for Seeed Studio XIAO *(~3€)*
- M3 screw set with nuts and screwdriver/ratchet wrench *(~8€ for the screw set)*, you need:
  - 24x M3x12 (16 with flats)
  - 12x M3x16 with flats
  - 6x M3x30
  - 12x locks
- M3 silicon shock absorber rings *(~3€)*, you need 18 rings
- 3D printer for the frame (Elegoo Neptune 4) *(~200€)* 
- White & black Elegoo Pla+ *(~20€ per spool)*
- soldering equipment: 
  - basic soldering iron *(~20€)*
  - 900M-T-I soldering tips recommend when working with the ESP32 & sensors *(~5€)*
  - brass wool for cleaning *(~5-10€)*
  - tip re-tiner *(~5€)*
- basic multimeter *(~15-20€)*
- high AWG cables, e.g. breadboard jumper wires
- crimping pliers with JST-XH 2.5mm connectors *(~25€ for a full set)*
- double sided tape

Most of the items I got from AliExpress.
